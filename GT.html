1.GILL

  • Gujarat Titans (often abbreviated as GT) is a franchise cricket team based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India. The Titans compete in the Indian Premier League (IPL).
  • Founded in 2021, Gujarat Titans' home ground is Narendra Modi Stadium in Motera.
  • The Governing Council of the Indian Premier League issued an invitation to tender for two new sides in August 2021.[6] A total of 22 companies declared an interest, but with a high base price for the new teams, there were no more than six serious bidders.
  • Ahead of the IPL 2022 mega auction, the franchise drafted Hardik Pandya as their captain. The franchise also picked Shubman Gill and Rashid Khan.[4] The Ahmedabad franchise revealed the name of its cricket team as the Gujarat Titans on 9 February 2022.[10][11]
